Bloopworld Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

  • We use Google for authentication.
  • We store your email and a Google-provided identifier to verify account ownership.
  • An authentication cookie is stored locally on your device.

2. Use of Data

  • Your email is never used for contact or shared with third parties.
  • We collect gameplay and usage metrics.
  • We retain in-game data and may use it publicly or privately for display or analysis.
  • In-game data does not include personal data, which is never disclosed.
  • Player usernames are considered in-game data.

3. No Ads or Sharing

  • We do not show ads or engage in targeted advertising.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

4. Contact Us

  • If you have any concerns, please email bloopworldgame@gmail.com. We will do our best to address any issues promptly.
